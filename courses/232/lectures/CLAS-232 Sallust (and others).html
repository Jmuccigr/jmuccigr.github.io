C. Sallustius Crispus

Life

  • Lived 86 c.35 BC

Career

Popularis politician and novus homo from a provincial plebeian family of Amiternum

  • Tribune of the Plebs in 52 BC, when followers of Milo killed Clodius in a street brawl; supported the prosecution of Milo
  • Censor Appius Claudius Pulcher removed him from the Senate in 50 BC, on the grounds of gross immorality
  • 49 re-instated probably due to Caesar
  • 49 Moved to Illyricum and probably commanded at least one legion
  • 48 Perhaps moved back to Rome and made quaestor to enter the senate
  • 46 Praetor, accompanied Caesar in his African campaign
  • Given governorship of Africa Nova for which he was prosecuted
  • Bought gardens in Rome & retired

Works

  • Catilines War (conspiracy in 63 BC of L. Sergius Catilina)
    • Not annalistic, but monogram on one topic
    • Strong moralizing
    • Fall of Rome begins after the defeat of Carthage and the lack of an external enemy
  • The Jugurthine War (war against the Numidians 111 to 105 BC)
    • Also monograph
  • Histories
    • fragmentary annals covering 78 to ???

Very influenced by the Greek historian Thucydides in his style and in turn influenced Tacitus
